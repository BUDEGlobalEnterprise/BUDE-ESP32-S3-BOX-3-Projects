## n8n Automation Ideas for Garden Projects

1. **Weather-Based Irrigation:** Fetch forecast → Skip watering if rain predicted
2. **Plant Health Alerts:** Camera detects disease → Telegram message with photo
3. **Harvest Reminders:** Growth tracker hits threshold → Email "Tomatoes ready!"
4. **Fertilizer Schedule:** Calendar automation → Notification + log application
5. **Water Bill Optimization:** Track usage → Generate cost reports → Adjust schedule
6. **Community Sharing:** Excess harvest detected → Post to neighborhood group
7. **Seed Planting Reminder:** Climate zone + calendar → "Plant carrots this week"
8. **Compost Readiness:** Temperature profile complete → "Spread compost now"

All workflows integrate with Metabase for analytics dashboards showing season-over-season improvements.
